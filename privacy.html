Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Expanding Land ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website expanding.land and related services including the Legend Mod browser extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile picture. We do not store your Google password.
  • Game Data: In-game statistics such as scores, mass, and gameplay metrics are collected to provide leaderboards and game features.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, and connection metadata are used for security, anti-cheat measures, and server performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and provide access to game features
  • To display your profile and scores on leaderboards
  • To maintain server security and prevent cheating
  • To improve our services and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth for authentication. When you sign in with Google, Google's Privacy Policy applies to the data they process. We only receive the information you authorize during sign-in.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS). We retain account data only as long as your account is active. Game statistics may be retained for leaderboard purposes.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of your account data at any time
  • You can revoke Google sign-in access from your Google Account settings
  • You can play as a guest without signing in

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Discord server.

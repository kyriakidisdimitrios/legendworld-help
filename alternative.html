Expanding Land supports 1024 concurrent players per arena — that's more than 5× Agar.io's limit. And every single one is a real person. Fair Play: Multiboxing & No-Bots Policy

Expanding Land takes a unique approach to fairness. Bots are strictly prohibited — there are no AI opponents, no bot accounts, and automated play is detected and blocked. Every cell you encounter is controlled by a real human.

Multiboxing is allowed but regulated: each player can run up to 4 accounts simultaneously. This gives skilled players the ability to multibox strategically, while preventing the kind of mass-bot abuse that plagues Agar.io. It's a balance between freedom and fairness — real skill, not scripts.
